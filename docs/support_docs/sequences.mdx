# Sequences

Create automated email sequences that nurture leads and drive conversions. Build from templates or create custom flows.

<Info>
  Sequences are a **Pro-only feature**. Upgrade to Pro to access email automation and marketing sequences.
</Info>

## What Are Sequences?

Sequences are automated series of emails (or other content) sent over time.

**Common Sequence Types:**
- **Welcome Sequences** - Onboard new subscribers
- **Nurture Sequences** - Build trust and educate
- **Sales Sequences** - Convert leads to customers
- **Webinar Sequences** - Pre/post webinar engagement
- **Launch Sequences** - Product/service launches

**Why Sequences Work:**
- Automate follow-up (set it, forget it)
- Consistent touchpoints (no manual sending)
- Proven frameworks (templates based on what converts)
- Personalized at scale (AI + offer data)

---

## Sequence Types

### Welcome Sequence

First emails new subscribers receive.

**Purpose:** Deliver value, set expectations, build relationship

**Typical Flow:**
1. **Email 1** (Immediate) - Welcome + deliver lead magnet
2. **Email 2** (Day 2) - Your story (why you created this)
3. **Email 3** (Day 4) - Social proof (case study or testimonial)
4. **Email 4** (Day 6) - Helpful content (free value)
5. **Email 5** (Day 8) - Soft pitch (main offer)

**Average Open Rate:** 50-70% (Email 1), 25-40% (Email 5)

---

### Sales Sequence (Low Ticket)

Convert leads to customers for products under $500.

**Purpose:** Overcome objections, demonstrate value, create urgency

**Typical Flow:**
1. **Email 1** - Problem acknowledgment
2. **Email 2** - Solution introduction
3. **Email 3** - Social proof
4. **Email 4** - FAQ/Objection handling
5. **Email 5** - Urgency + final CTA

**Duration:** 5-7 days
**Average Conversion:** 2-5%

---

### Sales Sequence (High Ticket)

Convert leads to customers for products $500+.

**Purpose:** Build trust, educate, book calls

**Typical Flow:**
1. **Email 1** - Establish authority
2. **Email 2** - Case study (transformation story)
3. **Email 3** - Method overview (how it works)
4. **Email 4** - Objections (price, time, skepticism)
5. **Email 5** - Book a call CTA
6. **Email 6** - FAQ
7. **Email 7** - Final invitation

**Duration:** 7-14 days
**Goal:** Book qualified sales calls

---

### Nurture Sequence

Keep subscribers engaged without hard selling.

**Purpose:** Build trust, provide value, stay top-of-mind

**Typical Flow:**
1. **Email 1** - Helpful tip or hack
2. **Email 2** - Story or case study
3. **Email 3** - Industry insight
4. **Email 4** - Tool or resource recommendation
5. **Email 5** - Behind-the-scenes content
6. **Email 6** - Soft pitch (optional)
7. **Email 7** - Survey or question

**Duration:** Evergreen (ongoing)
**Frequency:** Weekly or bi-weekly

---

### Webinar Sequence

Before and after webinar engagement.

**Pre-Webinar Flow:**
1. **Email 1** (Immediate) - Confirmation + add to calendar
2. **Email 2** (1 day before) - Reminder + what to expect
3. **Email 3** (1 hour before) - Final reminder + join link

**Post-Webinar Flow:**
1. **Email 1** (Immediately after) - Replay link + special offer
2. **Email 2** (1 day later) - Reminder + deadline for offer
3. **Email 3** (2 days later) - Final chance + scarcity

**Conversion Window:** 48-72 hours post-webinar

---

### Launch Sequence

Product or service launch campaign.

**Purpose:** Build anticipation, create demand, drive sales

**Typical Flow:**
1. **Email 1** (7 days before) - Teaser announcement
2. **Email 2** (5 days before) - Problem deep-dive
3. **Email 3** (3 days before) - Solution preview
4. **Email 4** (Launch day) - Doors open announcement
5. **Email 5** (Day 2) - Social proof + bonuses
6. **Email 6** (Day 4) - Objection handling
7. **Email 7** (Day 6) - Final hours urgency

**Duration:** 7-10 days
**Peak Conversion:** Launch day + final day

## Building a Sequence

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Sequence">
    Dashboard > **Sequences** > **Create New Sequence**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Type">
    Select from templates (Welcome, Sales, Nurture, etc.) or start from scratch
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Offer Context">
    Choose which offer this sequence promotes (for personalization)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Template">
    See pre-filled email structure and timing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Emails">
    Edit subject lines, body copy, timing for each email
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Trigger">
    Define what starts the sequence (form submission, tag added, etc.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate">
    Turn on sequence to start sending
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Time to Build:** 15-30 minutes

### Variable Mapping

Templates use variables populated from your offer.

**Common Variables:**
```
{first_name} - Subscriber first name
{offer_name} - Your product/service name
{unique_benefit} - Main benefit from offer
{price} - Product price
{guarantee} - Money-back guarantee terms
{founder_name} - Your name
{company_name} - Business name
```

**Example:**
```
Subject: {first_name}, here's your {offer_name}

Hey {first_name},

Thanks for grabbing {offer_name}. Here's what to do next...
```

**AI Auto-Fills:** Variables pulled from offer document automatically

## Email Editor

### Writing Emails

Each email in sequence has:

**Subject Line:**
- 6-10 words ideal
- Curiosity or benefit-driven
- Personalization (use {first_name})
- No spam words (FREE, ACT NOW, !!!)

**Preheader Text:**
- 40-100 characters
- Complements subject line
- Shows in inbox preview

**Body Copy:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Clear CTA (one per email)
- Conversational tone
- White space for readability

**CTA Button:**
- Action-oriented text ("Get Started", "Book My Call")
- Linked to funnel or booking page
- Contrasting color

---

---

### Email Timing

Control when each email sends.

**Timing Options:**
- **Immediate** - As soon as trigger fires
- **Delay** - X hours or days after previous email
- **Specific Day** - Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, etc.
- **Specific Time** - 9 AM, 2 PM (subscriber timezone)

**Best Practices:**
- Email 1: Immediate (strike while iron is hot)
- Email 2-3: 2-3 days apart
- Email 4+: 3-5 days apart
- Avoid weekends for B2B

## Triggers & Activation

### Sequence Triggers

What starts a sequence?

**Trigger Types:**

**Form Submission:**
- Lead magnet opt-in
- Webinar registration
- Contact form

**Tag Added:**
- Manual tagging
- Integration trigger (Airtable, GHL)
- Funnel completion

**Funnel Action:**
- Visited specific page
- Abandoned cart
- Purchased product

**Manual:**
- Add contact to sequence manually

---

### Activation

Turn sequences on/off.

**Status Options:**
- **Draft** - Not sending (still building)
- **Active** - Sending to new subscribers
- **Paused** - Stops new enrollments, existing continue
- **Archived** - Inactive, hidden from main view

**How to Activate:**
1. Complete all emails
2. Configure trigger
3. Review sequence
4. Click **Activate**

Subscribers start receiving emails based on trigger.

### Editing Active Sequences

Can you edit after activation?

**Yes:**
- Edit existing emails (changes apply to new sends only)
- Add new emails to end
- Adjust timing

**No:**
- Remove emails (breaks sequence for active subscribers)
- Change trigger (affects tracking)

**Best Practice:** Duplicate sequence, edit copy, test new version

## Best Practices

### Subject Lines That Get Opened

**Do:**
- Use curiosity ("The mistake I see everyone make...")
- Ask questions ("Ready to 10x your leads?")
- Personalize ("{first_name}, this is for you")
- Be specific ("3 ways to fix your funnel")

**Don't:**
- ALL CAPS
- Excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Spam trigger words (FREE, ACT NOW)
- Generic ("Newsletter #47")

**Target Open Rate:** 25-40% (industry average)

---

### Copy That Converts

**Structure:**
1. **Hook** - First sentence grabs attention
2. **Value** - What they'll get from reading
3. **Story/Proof** - Example or case study
4. **CTA** - One clear action
5. **Sign-Off** - Your name, personal touch

**Tone:**
- Conversational (write like you talk)
- Short sentences and paragraphs
- Use "you" not "we"
- Active voice

---

### Timing & Frequency

**How Often:**
- Welcome sequence: Daily or every 2 days
- Sales sequence: Every 2-3 days
- Nurture: Weekly
- Launch: Daily during launch window

**Best Send Times:**
- **B2B:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM - 11 AM or 2 PM - 4 PM
- **B2C:** Evenings and weekends work better
- **E-commerce:** Late morning (10 AM - 12 PM)

**Test Your Audience:** What works varies by industry

---

### Deliverability

Keep emails out of spam.

**Do:**
- Use reputable email service (VibeOS handles this)
- Authenticate domain (SPF, DKIM records)
- Clean list regularly (remove bounces)
- Easy unsubscribe (required by law)
- Avoid spam words
- Balance text/images (60% text, 40% images)

**Don't:**
- Buy email lists
- Send without permission
- Hide unsubscribe link
- Use misleading subject lines

**Goal:** >95% deliverability rate

## Troubleshooting

### Emails Not Sending

**Check:**
- Sequence status = Active
- Trigger configured correctly
- Email addresses valid
- Not hitting send limits

**Fix:**
1. Verify sequence is active
2. Test trigger manually
3. Check email deliverability settings

---

### Low Open Rates

**Causes:**
- Weak subject lines
- Poor list quality
- Deliverability issues
- Wrong send time

**Fix:**
1. A/B test subject lines
2. Clean list (remove bounces)
3. Check spam folder placement
4. Test different send times

---

### High Unsubscribe Rate

**Normal:** 0.1-0.5% per email
**High:** 1%+

**Causes:**
- Sending too frequently
- Content not valuable
- Misleading opt-in
- Wrong audience

**Fix:**
1. Space emails further apart
2. Improve content quality
3. Re-evaluate offer/audience fit
