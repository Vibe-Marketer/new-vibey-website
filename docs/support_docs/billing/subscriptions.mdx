# Subscription Management

Manage your VibeOS subscription. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

## Current Plan

Check your current plan and usage:

**Settings > Billing > Current Plan**

You'll see:
- Plan type (Free Trial, Free, or Pro)
- Trial status (if applicable)
- Billing cycle (Monthly, Annual, or N/A for Free)
- Next billing date
- Usage metrics (published funnels, AI words, storage, domains)

---

## Understanding Trials

VibeOS offers two trial options to help you explore Pro features.

### Free Trial (7 Days)

**Perfect for:** Testing VibeOS with zero risk

**Details:**
- **Duration:** 7 days
- **Cost:** $0 (no credit card required)
- **Access:** Full PRO features
- **AI Words:** 50,000 during trial
- **After Trial:** Automatically downgrades to Free plan

**How to Start:**
1. Sign up for VibeOS
2. Select "Start Free Trial"
3. No payment required
4. Explore for 7 days

**What Happens After 7 Days:**
- Account downgrades to Free plan
- Keeps 3 published funnels (10 total drafts)
- AI words drop to 10,000/month
- All data retained
- Can upgrade to Pro anytime

### Paid Trial (30 Days)

**Perfect for:** Building and launching with extended time

**Details:**
- **Duration:** 30 days
- **Cost:** $1 (credit card required)
- **Access:** Full PRO features
- **AI Words:** 50,000 during trial
- **After Trial:** Automatically converts to $147/month (cancel anytime)

**How to Start:**
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Select "Upgrade to Pro"
3. Choose "$1 Trial (30 days)"
4. Enter payment method
5. Charged $1 immediately

**What Happens After 30 Days:**
- Automatically converts to Pro at $147/month
- Full PRO access continues
- Cancel before 30 days to avoid charge
- If canceled, downgrades to Free plan

### Which Trial Should You Choose?

**Choose 7-Day Free Trial If:**
- You want to explore VibeOS risk-free
- No credit card handy
- Just testing features
- Not ready to commit

**Choose 30-Day $1 Trial If:**
- You want to build and launch
- Need more time to evaluate
- Ready to potentially commit to Pro
- Want extended PRO access for just $1

## Upgrading to Pro

Ready for more? Upgrade anytime.

### From Free Trial to Pro

After your 7-day free trial ends:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Upgrade to Pro**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Option">
    - **$1 Trial (30 days):** Pay $1, get 30 more days, then $147/month
    - **Monthly:** $147/month starting immediately
    - **Annual:** $1,497/year (save $267)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Enter credit card details. Processed securely through Stripe.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm & Pay">
    Review and confirm. Charged immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From Free Plan to Pro

If you're on the free forever plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Upgrade to Pro**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Billing Cycle">
    - **$1 Trial (30 days):** Pay $1, then $147/month after 30 days
    - **Monthly:** $147/month starting immediately
    - **Annual:** $1,497/year (save $267)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Enter credit card details. Processed securely through Stripe.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm & Pay">
    Review and confirm. Charged immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What Happens After Upgrade:**
- Pro features activate instantly
- Limits increase immediately (3 → 10 published funnels, 10K → 50K AI words, etc.)
- Campaigns, sequences, and integrations unlocked
- Custom domains available (up to 5)
- All existing data stays intact

### Mid-Month Upgrade

Upgrade in the middle of the month? No problem.

**How Billing Works:**
1. Charged prorated amount for remainder of month
2. Full monthly charge on your next billing date
3. That date becomes your recurring billing date

**Example:**
- You upgrade on Jan 15
- Monthly plan: $1 trial for first month
- On Feb 15, you pay $147
- Then billed $147 on 15th of each month

## Switching Billing Cycles

### Monthly to Annual

Want to save 37%? Switch to annual billing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cancel Monthly Plan">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Cancel Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Subscribe to Annual">
    Click **Upgrade to Pro** > Choose **Annual**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pay">
    You're charged $1,497 immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  No double-charging. When you cancel monthly, it ends immediately, and annual starts fresh.
</Info>

### Annual to Monthly

Switching to monthly means waiting for annual plan to expire.

**Process:**
1. Cancel annual plan (ends at 12-month mark)
2. Wait for expiration
3. Subscribe to monthly plan

OR

1. Cancel annual plan (get prorated refund for unused months)
2. Immediately subscribe to monthly plan

**Which Is Better?**
- If 8+ months left: Cancel and get refund, subscribe monthly
- If &lt;8 months left: Wait for expiration

## Downgrading from Pro to Free

Need to cut costs? Downgrade anytime.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Manage Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cancel Pro Subscription">
    Click **Cancel Subscription** > Confirm
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Downgrade Reason">
    Optional feedback helps us improve
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Account moves to Free plan immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Happens When You Downgrade

**Immediately:**
- Limits drop to Free plan (3 offers, 3 funnels, 1GB storage)
- Chat Copilot access removed (history saved)
- No more charges
- Priority support ends

**Your Data:**
- First 3 offers stay active
- First 3 funnels stay active
- Only 1 funnel can be published (extras unpublish automatically)
- Leads and CRM data retained
- Media over 1GB becomes read-only (delete files to free space)
- Analytics retained (30 days rolling window)

**Which Data Gets Unpublished?**
- Oldest funnels unpublished first (by creation date)
- You can choose which funnel to keep published

### Choosing What to Keep

Before downgrading, you can manually pick what stays:

1. Delete offers/funnels you don't need
2. Export important offers as DOCX
3. Choose which funnel to keep published
4. Then downgrade

## Canceling Your Subscription

Cancel anytime. No penalties. No hoops.

### How to Cancel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Cancel Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Click **Cancel Subscription** > Confirm
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional Feedback">
    Tell us why you're canceling (helps us improve)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Done">
    Subscription canceled. Account moves to Free plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### When Cancellation Takes Effect

**Monthly Plans:**
- Cancels immediately
- No refund for partial month
- No more charges

**Annual Plans:**
- Cancels immediately
- Prorated refund for unused months (after 7-day guarantee)
- Example: 8 months unused = ~$998 refund

### What You Keep After Canceling

- Your account (stays active on Free plan)
- 3 offers
- 3 funnels (1 published)
- 1GB storage
- All leads and contacts
- 30-day analytics history
- All integrations

### What You Lose After Canceling

- Chat Copilot access (conversations saved, can't create new)
- Extra funnel slots (only 1 published)
- Extra storage (read-only until you delete files)
- Priority support
- 90-day analytics (drops to 30-day)

## Refund Policy

### 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

First-time Pro subscribers get full refund within 7 days.

**Eligibility:**
- First Pro subscription only
- Request within 7 calendar days of charge
- No usage limits—use it fully, still get refund

**How to Request:**
Email billing@govibey.com with subject "Refund Request"

**Processing:**
- Approved within 1-2 business days
- Refund arrives in 5-10 business days (depends on bank)
- Account downgrades to Free immediately

### Prorated Refunds (Annual Plans)

Annual plan canceled after 7 days? You get prorated refund.

**Formula:**
```
Refund = (Unused Months / 12) × $1,497
```

**Example:**
- Paid $1,497 for annual plan on Jan 1
- Canceled on April 1 (3 months used, 9 remaining)
- Refund: (9 / 12) × $1,497 = **$1,122.75**

**Processing:**
- Automatic calculation
- Refund issued within 5-7 business days
- Arrives in 5-10 days via original payment method

### No Refunds

**Monthly Plans:**
- No refunds after 7-day guarantee period
- Cancel anytime to avoid future charges

**Partial Months:**
- Monthly plans don't refund partial months
- If you cancel on the 10th, no refund for remaining 20 days

## Automatic Renewals

### How Auto-Renewal Works

All subscriptions auto-renew by default.

**Monthly Plans:**
- Renew same day each month
- Example: Subscribed Jan 10 → Renews Feb 10, Mar 10, etc.

**Annual Plans:**
- Renew same day each year
- Example: Subscribed Jan 10, 2025 → Renews Jan 10, 2026

### Renewal Reminders

We email you before each renewal:

- **7 days before** monthly renewal
- **30 days before** annual renewal

Email includes:
- Renewal date
- Amount to be charged
- Payment method used
- Link to cancel if needed

### Disabling Auto-Renewal

Can't disable auto-renewal. Cancel subscription instead (ends at period end).

**For Monthly:** Cancel → No more charges → Downgrades to Free
**For Annual:** Cancel → Get prorated refund → Downgrades to Free

## Subscription FAQs

**Can I upgrade and downgrade multiple times?**
Yes. No limits. Charges or refunds apply accordingly.

**If I downgrade, can I upgrade later without losing data?**
Yes. All data stays for 90 days (even deleted accounts). Upgrade anytime.

**What happens to Chat Copilot conversations when I cancel?**
Conversations stay in your account. You can read them but can't create new ones until you upgrade again.

**Can I transfer my subscription to someone else?**
Not yet. Contact billing@govibey.com for special cases (business acquisitions, etc.).

**Do unused AI chat words roll over after downgrade?**
No. Words reset when you cancel. Fresh 50K on upgrade to Pro.

**Can I cancel annual plan mid-year?**
Yes. You get prorated refund for unused months.

**How do I know when my next charge is?**
Settings > **Billing** > Next Billing Date

**What if I forget to cancel and get charged?**
Email billing@govibey.com. If it's within 7 days, we issue full refund. After 7 days, no refunds for monthly plans.

**Can I get a discount if I subscribe for 2 years?**
Not currently. Annual plan (37% savings) is our best rate.
