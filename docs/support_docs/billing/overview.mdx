## Need More AI Words?

If you hit your monthly AI word limit, you have options:

**Word Packs (All Users):**
- Purchase additional AI words anytime
- **$10 per 25,000 words**
- Never expires
- Available to both Free and Pro users
- Stack multiple packs
- Automatically deducted before monthly allowance

**Example:**
- You're on Free plan (10,000 words/month)
- Purchase 2 word packs (50,000 words total for $20)
- You now have 50,000 words that never expire + 10,000 words/month

**Free Users:**
- Buy word packs as needed ($10/25K words)
- OR upgrade to Pro for 5x more words (50,000/month)
- Monthly words reset on 1st of each month

**Pro Users:**
- 50,000 words/month included
- Buy word packs for extra capacity ($10/25K words)
- Word packs never expire
- Monthly allowance resets on 1st of each month

<Info>
  Word packs are one-time purchases that never expire. Your monthly allowance (Free: 10K, Pro: 50K) resets every month. Word packs are used first, then monthly allowance.
</Info>

## Limits & Usage

Track your usage in Settings > Billing.

### How Limits Work

**Soft Limits:**
- You can't publish more funnels than your plan allows
- You can't add more domains than your plan allows
- You can't generate offers beyond your limit

**Storage:**
- Upload blocked when you hit storage limit
- Delete old media to free up space

**AI Words (Free & Pro):**
- Stops working when you hit word limit
- Resets on 1st of each month
- Free: 10,000 words/month
- Pro: 50,000 words/month

### Checking Usage

Settings > **Billing** > View Usage

You'll see:
- **Published Funnels:** 2/3 (Free) or 8/10 (Pro)
- **Total Funnels (Drafts):** 5/10 (Free) or 15/unlimited (Pro)
- **Storage:** 230MB / 1GB (Free) or 3.5GB / 10GB (Pro)
- **AI Words (Monthly):** 5,000 / 10,000 (Free) or 25,000 / 50,000 (Pro)
- **Word Packs (Purchased):** 12,500 remaining (if purchased)
- **Custom Domains:** 0/0 (Free) or 2/5 (Pro)

## Upgrading Your Plan

### From Free Trial to Pro

After your 7-day free trial ends, you can upgrade anytime:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Upgrade to Pro**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Billing Cycle">
    - **$1 Trial (30 days) + Monthly**: $1 for 30 days, then $147/month
    - **Annual**: $1,497/year (save $267 vs monthly)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Enter credit card details (Stripe secure checkout)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and confirm. Charged immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Pro features activate instantly.

### From Free Plan to Pro

If you're on the free forever plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Settings > **Billing** > **Upgrade to Pro**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Trial Option">
    - **30-Day $1 Trial**: Test Pro for 30 days at $1, then $147/month
    - **Skip Trial - Monthly**: $147/month starting immediately
    - **Skip Trial - Annual**: $1,497/year (save $267)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Enter credit card details (Stripe secure checkout)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate">
    Pro features unlock instantly
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Happens to Your Data

Nothing changes. All your offers, funnels, leads, and media stay exactly the same. You just get higher limits and new features.

## Downgrading Your Plan

### From Pro to Free

You can downgrade anytime. Here's what happens:

**Immediately:**
- Limits drop to Free plan levels
- Chat Copilot access removed (chat history saved)
- No more charges

**Your Data:**
- First 3 **published** funnels stay active
- Extra published funnels automatically unpublished (funnels 4-10 become drafts)
- All 10 funnel drafts retained
- Vibey branding added back to funnels and exports
- Leads and analytics retained
- Storage over 1GB: Read-only until you delete files
- Campaigns and sequences disabled
- Integrations disabled
- Custom domains disconnected
- Word packs retained (never expire)

**To Downgrade:**
Settings > **Billing** > **Cancel Subscription**

---

## Canceling Your Subscription

Cancel anytime. No penalties.

<Warning>
  If you downgrade or cancel, you lose access to Pro features immediately. Your data stays, but extra funnels will unpublish.
</Warning>

**What's Deleted:**
- Nothing. Your account stays active on Free plan.

**What You Lose:**
- AI word allowance drops to 10,000/month (word packs stay)
- Extra published funnel slots (beyond 3 published, keep all 10 drafts)
- Campaigns and sequences access
- Integration access
- Custom domain access (up to 5 domains disconnected)
- Unbranded exports (Vibey branding added back)
- Extra storage (beyond 1GB becomes read-only)
- Priority support

**To Cancel:**
Settings > **Billing** > **Cancel Subscription** > Confirm

## Billing FAQs

**Can I switch between monthly and annual?**
Yes. Cancel your current plan and subscribe to the other. No double-charging.

**Do you offer discounts?**
Annual plan saves you $267/year. We occasionally run quarterly payment promotions—join [community](https://skool.com/vibemarketing) for announcements.

**Can I pause my subscription?**
Not yet. Cancel and restart when ready. Data stays for 90 days.

**What if my payment fails?**
We retry 3 times over 7 days. If still failing, account downgrades to Free. Update payment method to restore.

**Is there a setup fee?**
No. Monthly starts with $1 trial, then $147/month. Annual is $1,497/year.

**Can I upgrade mid-month?**
Yes. Monthly: $1 trial, then $147 on next cycle. Annual: $1,497 charged immediately.

**Do you offer quarterly billing?**
Occasionally as a special promotion. Watch for announcements.
