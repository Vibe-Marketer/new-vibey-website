# Funnels

Build complete marketing funnels in minutes. Choose templates, generate AI copy from your offer, customize, and publish.

## What Is a Funnel?

A funnel is a series of pages designed to convert visitors into leads or customers.

**Typical Funnel Flow:**
1. **Landing Page** - Visitor arrives, sees your offer
2. **Opt-in Page** - Captures email/contact info
3. **Confirmation Page** - Confirms submission, sets expectations
4. **Thank You Page** - Delivers value, next steps

Some funnels include additional pages like sales pages, booking pages, or replay pages.

---

## Funnel Types

Vibey supports 3 main funnel types:

### Lead Magnet Funnel

Capture leads with valuable free content.

**Pages Included:**
- Opt-in page (capture email)
- Confirmation page
- Thank you page (deliver lead magnet)

**Best For:**
- eBooks, guides, templates
- Checklists, cheat sheets
- Free courses or trainings
- Resource libraries

**Average Conversion:** 30-50%

---

### Webinar Funnel

Register attendees for live or automated webinars.

**Pages Included:**
- Registration page (opt-in)
- Confirmation page (add to calendar)
- Offer page (pitch after webinar)
- Replay page (for those who missed it)

**Best For:**
- Educational webinars
- Product demos
- Workshops and masterclasses
- Automated evergreen webinars

**Average Conversion:** 40-60% (registration), 20-30% (show-up rate)

---

### Call Booking Funnel

Pre-qualify leads and book calls.

**Pages Included:**
- Pre-call questionnaire (qualify leads)
- Booking page (calendar integration)
- Confirmation page (call details)

**Best For:**
- Sales calls
- Consulting calls
- Strategy sessions
- Discovery calls

**Average Conversion:** 20-40% (depending on qualification)

## Building Your First Funnel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Funnel">
    Dashboard > **Funnels** > **Create New Funnel**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Funnel Type">

  </Step>

  <Step title="Name Your Funnel">
    Give it a descriptive name (only you see this)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Templates">
    Choose template for each page (you can preview before selecting)

  <Step title="Generate Copy">
    Connect your offer. Vibey writes all copy automatically based on your offer document and brand guidelines.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit text, images, colors, layout using the visual editor
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish">
    Choose domain (Vibey subdomain or custom domain) and go live
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Total Time:** 10-20 minutes

## Page Types Explained

### Opt-in Pages

Capture email addresses in exchange for value.

**Elements:**
- Strong headline (benefit-driven)
- Subheadline (clarify offer)
- Bullet points (what they get)
- Email capture form
- CTA button ("Get Instant Access")
- Social proof (optional)
- Hero image or video

**Best Practices:**
- Keep form fields minimal (email only or email + name)
- Clear value proposition above fold
- Remove navigation (keep focus on opt-in)

---

### Confirmation Pages

Confirm submission and set expectations.

**Elements:**
- "Success!" headline
- What happens next (check email)
- When to expect delivery
- Additional CTA (join community, follow social, etc.)

**Best Practices:**
- Confirm action immediately
- Set clear expectations ("Check your email in 5 minutes")
- Offer secondary CTA (low friction)

---

### Thank You Pages

Deliver value and guide next steps.

**Elements:**
- Thank you headline
- Delivery confirmation (download link, access instructions)
- Next steps (consume content, schedule call, etc.)
- Optional upsell or additional offer

**Best Practices:**
- Make delivery instant and obvious
- Guide them to action ("Watch this video now")
- Include bonus content to increase perceived value

---

### Sales/Offer Pages

Pitch your product or service.

**Elements:**
- Strong headline (outcome-focused)
- Problem-solution-outcome framework
- Features and benefits
- Social proof (testimonials, case studies)
- Pricing and guarantee
- CTA buttons (multiple throughout page)

**Best Practices:**
- Lead with outcome, not features
- Use story structure (before/after)
- Include FAQ section
- Multiple CTAs (every screen height)

---

### Booking Pages

Integrate calendar for scheduling.

**Elements:**
- Calendar embed (Calendly, Cal.com)
- What to expect on call
- Preparation instructions
- CTA ("Choose Your Time")

**Best Practices:**
- Pre-qualify before showing calendar
- Set expectations (call length, format)
- Confirm timezone automatically

## Publishing Your Funnel

### Publish to Vibey Subdomain

Every account gets a free Vibey subdomain.

**Format:** `yourname.vibeos.com`

**How to Set Up:**
1. Funnel editor > **Publish**
2. Choose **Vibey Subdomain**
3. Enter your subdomain name (must be unique)
4. Publish

Live in 30 seconds.

---

### Publish to Custom Domain

Use your own domain for full branding.

**Requirements:**
- Add and verify domain in Settings (see [Domains guide](/domains))
- DNS records configured
- Domain status = "Verified"

**How to Set Up:**
1. Funnel editor > **Publish**
2. Choose **Custom Domain**
3. Select from your verified domains
4. Publish

Automatic HTTPS included.

## Managing Funnels

### Viewing All Funnels

Dashboard > **Funnels** > See all funnels

**You'll See:**
- Funnel name
- Funnel type (Lead Magnet, Webinar, etc.)
- Status (Draft, Published)
- Domain (where it's published)
- Last edited date
- View count and conversion rate

### Unpublishing a Funnel

Funnels > Select funnel > **Unpublish**

Funnel goes offline immediately. URL returns 404.

**Use Cases:**
- Campaign ended
- Need to make major edits
- Seasonal offer (pause until next season)

**Data Stays:** Leads captured before unpublishing remain in your CRM.

### Duplicating a Funnel

Create variations or A/B tests:

1. Funnels > Select funnel
2. Click **Duplicate**
3. New copy created
4. Edit and publish to different URL

## Optimization Tips

### Above the Fold

First screen matters most.

**What to Include:**
- Clear headline (outcome-focused)
- Subheadline (1-2 sentences)
- Primary CTA button
- Hero image or video

**What to Skip:**
- Navigation menu
- Long paragraphs
- Multiple CTAs

### Social Proof

Build trust with evidence.

**Types of Social Proof:**
- Testimonials (quotes from customers)
- Case studies (results with numbers)
- Logos (companies you've worked with)
- Stats (users, results, ratings)

**Where to Place:**
- Near CTA buttons
- Mid-page (after problem/solution)
- Footer (trust badges)

### Mobile Optimization

50%+ of traffic is mobile.

**Vibey Auto-Optimizes:**
- Responsive layouts
- Mobile-friendly fonts
- Touch-friendly buttons
- Fast load times

**You Should:**
- Test on actual mobile device
- Keep copy short (half of desktop)
- Use vertical images (not horizontal)

## Troubleshooting

### Funnel Won't Publish

**Check:**
- All required fields filled
- Domain verified (if using custom domain)
- Not at published funnel limit

**Fix:**
1. Review error message
2. Fix missing fields
3. Try publishing again

### Copy Generation Fails

**Cause:** No offer selected or API timeout

**Fix:**
1. Select an offer document
2. Make sure offer is complete
3. Retry generation
4. If still fails, write copy manually

### Leads Not Capturing

**Check:**
- Form is connected to CRM
- Email field is required
- Form submission tracking enabled

**Fix:**
1. Test form yourself
2. Check CRM for test lead
3. Verify integrations are connected
4. Contact support if issue persists
