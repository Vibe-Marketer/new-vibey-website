# Features Overview

VibeOS is a complete marketing operating system. Here's everything you can do.

## Core Features

### Offer Generation

Create comprehensive offer documents with AI.

**What You Get:**
- 6-step AI wizard
- Product and market analysis
- Power offer statement
- Buyer persona with demographics and psychographics
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Competitive edge analysis

**How It Works:**
1. Provide product URL and competitor URLs
2. AI scrapes and analyzes content
3. Answer guided questions
4. AI generates complete offer document
5. Export as DOCX or use in funnels

**Use Cases:**
- Launch new products
- Refine existing positioning
- Build lead magnets
- Create course outlines
- Develop service packages

**Limits:**
- Free: Unlimited exports with Vibey branding (PDF/Google Doc)
- Pro: Unlimited exports without branding

### Funnel Builder

Build complete marketing funnels in minutes.

**Funnel Types:**
- **Lead Magnet** - Opt-in page, confirmation page, thank you page
- **Webinar** - Registration, confirmation, offer, replay
- **Call Booking** - Pre-call questionnaire, booking page, confirmation

**Features:**
- Template library (constantly expanding)
- AI copy generation from your offer
- Visual editor with autosave
- Mobile-responsive designs
- Custom domains or Vibey subdomains
- Lead capture forms
- Integration with scheduling and CRM

**Building Process:**
1. Choose funnel type
2. Select template for each page
3. Generate AI copy from offer
4. Customize design and content
5. Publish to domain

**Limits:**
- Free: 3 published funnels (10 total drafts, on whitelabel subdomain)
- Pro: 10 published funnels (unlimited drafts)

### Custom Domains

Connect your domain. Automatic SSL/HTTPS.

**Features:**
- Unlimited custom domains per published funnel
- Automatic SSL certificate provisioning
- DNS verification wizard
- Support for apex and www subdomains
- Real-time status monitoring
- Multiple domain providers supported (Cloudflare, GoDaddy, Namecheap, Route 53, etc.)

**Setup Process:**
1. Add domain in Settings
2. Configure DNS records at provider
3. Verify (5-60 minutes)
4. Assign to funnel
5. Auto HTTPS enabled

**Limits:**
- Free: 0 custom domains (Pro required)
- Pro: 5 custom domains

## Pro-Only Features

### Vibey Chat (AI Copilot)

AI marketing assistant that knows your business.

**What It Does:**
- Answer marketing questions
- Brainstorm campaign ideas
- Critique funnel copy
- Analyze uploaded documents and templates
- Suggest improvements
- Explain marketing concepts

**How It Works:**
- Context-aware based on your offers
- Pulls from your brand guidelines
- Chat with uploaded documents and templates
- Suggests templates and tactics
- Conversational interface
- Powered by OpenRouter with caching (75-90% cost reduction)

**AI Word Calculation:**
```
AI Words = (input_tokens + output_tokens - cached_tokens) / 1000 * 750
```

Caching means repeated conversations about the same offer/document use fewer AI words.

**Limits:**
- Free: 10,000 AI words/month
- Pro: 50,000 AI words/month
- **Word Packs:** Purchase 25,000 words for $10 (never expires)

**Available to:** Free and Pro users
