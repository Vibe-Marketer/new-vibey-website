# Payments & Stripe

Accept payments directly through your funnels. One-time purchases, subscriptions, and payment plans—all powered by Stripe.

## Overview

Vibey integrates with Stripe Connect for seamless payment collection.

**What You Can Do:**
- Collect one-time payments
- Set up recurring subscriptions
- Offer payment plans
- Create discount coupons
- Manage refunds
- Customer portal for subscription management

**Requirements:**
- Stripe account (free to create)
- Business information (for verification)
- Bank account for payouts

---

## Getting Started

### Connect Stripe Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Settings > **Integrations** > **Stripe**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click **Connect Stripe Account**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Stripe Onboarding">
    You'll be redirected to Stripe. Complete these steps:
    - Create Stripe account (or login)
    - Provide business information
    - Add bank account for payouts
    - Submit tax information
    - Verify identity
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to Vibey">
    After approval, you're redirected back to Vibey with "Connected" status
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Onboarding Time:** 5-10 minutes
**Verification:** Usually instant (sometimes 1-2 business days)

---

### Stripe Account Types

**Individual:**
- Sole proprietor
- Freelancer
- Creator

**Business:**
- LLC, Corporation, Partnership
- Requires EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- More verification required

**Which to Choose:**
- Start with Individual if unsure
- Upgrade to Business later if needed

## Products & Services

### Creating Products

Define what you're selling.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Products">
    Settings > **Integrations** > **Stripe** > **Products**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Product">
    Click **Add Product**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Product name (e.g., "Coaching Package")
    - Description (customer-facing)
    - Product type (digital, service, physical)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Product created, ready for pricing
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Product Types:**
- **Digital:** eBooks, courses, templates
- **Service:** Coaching, consulting, design work
- **Physical:** Shipped products (requires shipping integration)

---

### Setting Prices

Products can have multiple price points.

**Price Options:**

**One-Time Payment:**
- Customer pays once
- Immediate access
- Example: $497 for course

**Recurring Subscription:**
- Customer pays regularly
- Options: Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly
- Example: $99/month for membership

**Payment Plan:**
- Split one-time price into payments
- Example: $1,497 paid as 3 x $499/month
- (Set up as recurring, cancel after X payments)

**Creating a Price:**
1. Products > Select Product > **Add Price**
2. Enter amount ($)
3. Choose billing period (one-time or recurring)
4. Set currency (USD, EUR, etc.)
5. Save

**Multiple Prices:**
- Monthly: $99/mo
- Annual: $997/year (save $191)
- Give customers options

## Coupons & Discounts

### Creating Coupons

Offer discounts to customers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Coupons">
    Settings > **Integrations** > **Stripe** > **Coupons**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Coupon">
    Click **Add Coupon**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Discount">
    - Coupon code (e.g., LAUNCH50)
    - Discount type: Percentage or fixed amount
    - Amount (50% or $100 off)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Duration">
    - Once (first payment only)
    - Forever (every payment)
    - Repeating (X months)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Limits (Optional)">
    - Max redemptions (e.g., first 50 customers)
    - Expiration date
    - One per customer
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Coupon code active
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Use Cases:**
- Launch discounts (LAUNCH50)
- Referral codes (REFER20)
- Seasonal sales (BLACKFRIDAY)
- Influencer partnerships (PARTNER100)

---

### Applying Coupons

**In Payment Links:**
- Include coupon in URL: `?coupon=LAUNCH50`
- Auto-applied at checkout

**In Funnel Checkout:**
- Customer enters code at checkout
- Discount shows before payment

## Payment Links

### Creating Payment Links

Shareable checkout links for your products.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payment Links">
    Settings > **Integrations** > **Stripe** > **Payment Links**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Link">
    Click **Create Payment Link**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Product & Price">
    Choose what to sell and which price point
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Options">
    - Allow coupon codes? (yes/no)
    - Collect shipping address? (if physical)
    - Custom success message
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Link">
    Get shareable URL (e.g., `pay.stripe.com/abc123xyz`)
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Where to Use:**
- Email directly to customers
- Share on social media
- Embed in funnels (button or iframe)
- Include in invoices

**Benefits:**
- No funnel needed
- Instant setup
- Fully hosted by Stripe
- Mobile-optimized

## Checkout Sessions

### Adding Checkout to Funnels

Embed payment checkout in your funnels.

**Setup:**
1. Funnel editor > Add **Payment Block**
2. Select Stripe product
3. Choose price
4. Customize button text
5. Configure success/cancel URLs
6. Save

**Checkout Options:**

**Embedded Checkout:**
- Checkout form within your funnel page
- Customer never leaves your site
- Best user experience

**Redirect Checkout:**
- Button redirects to Stripe-hosted checkout
- Easier setup
- Slightly lower conversion

**Cancel Page:**

## Subscription Management

### Customer Portal

Let customers manage their own subscriptions.

**Portal Features:**
- View subscription details
- Update payment method
- Cancel subscription
- View invoices
- Download receipts

**How to Enable:**
1. Stripe Dashboard > **Settings** > **Customer Portal**
2. Configure options (what customers can do)
3. Get portal link
4. Add to funnel or emails

**Link Format:** `billing.stripe.com/p/login/abc123xyz`

**Use Cases:**
- "Manage Subscription" button in member area
- Link in confirmation emails
- Self-service cancellation (reduces support)

---

### Handling Subscriptions

**Active Subscriptions:**
- Charged automatically on billing cycle
- Renewal emails sent (from Stripe)
- Payments recorded in Stripe dashboard

**Failed Payments:**
- Stripe retries automatically (up to 4 times)
- Customer emailed about failure
- Subscription paused/cancelled if payment doesn't succeed

**Cancellations:**
- Customer cancels via portal
- Or you cancel in Stripe dashboard
- Access continues until end of billing period
- No refunds (unless you issue manually)

## Refunds

### Issuing Refunds

**Full Refund:**
1. Stripe Dashboard > **Payments**
2. Find payment
3. Click **Refund**
4. Select "Full refund"
5. Confirm

**Partial Refund:**
- Same process, enter partial amount
- Example: Refund $50 of $497 payment

**Refund Timeline:**
- Credit cards: 5-10 business days
- Debit cards: 5-10 business days
- Depends on customer's bank

**Stripe Fees:**
- Stripe fees not refunded
- You eat the fee cost on refunds

---

### Refund Policy

Set clear expectations.

**Recommended Policy:**
- 7-day money-back guarantee
- 30-day for higher ticket
- No refunds for digital products after access granted

**Where to Display:**
- Checkout page (before payment)
- Terms of service
- FAQ page
- Confirmation emails

## Payment Security

### PCI Compliance

Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified (highest standard).

**What This Means:**
- Card details never touch your servers
- Vibey never sees full card numbers
- Encrypted data in transit
- Secure token storage

**Your Responsibility:**
- Don't store card details yourself
- Use Stripe's hosted checkout or embedded form
- Follow Stripe's security guidelines

---

### Fraud Prevention

Stripe's built-in fraud detection.

**Stripe Radar:**
- Machine learning fraud detection
- Blocks suspicious transactions
- 3D Secure for high-risk payments
- Automatically enabled

**Dispute Management:**
- Chargebacks handled through Stripe
- Upload evidence via Stripe Dashboard
- Stripe fights disputes for you (with evidence)

## Payouts

### How Payouts Work

**Payout Schedule:**
- **Default:** Daily (for most businesses)
- **Can Change To:** Weekly or monthly

**Payout Timing:**
- First payout: 7-14 days (verification period)
- After that: 2 business days after payment received
- Example: Payment on Monday → Payout Wednesday

**Bank Account:**
- Must verify bank account
- Only one bank account per Stripe account
- Change in Stripe Dashboard > **Settings** > **Payouts**

---

### Viewing Payouts

**Stripe Dashboard:**
- **Balance** > **Payouts**
- See all past payouts
- Upcoming payout amount
- Download payout statements

**Transaction Fees:**
- Stripe takes 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Deducted before payout
- Example: $100 sale → You receive $96.80

## Limits & Fees

### Stripe Fees

**Standard Pricing:**
- 2.9% + $0.30 per successful card charge
- No setup fees
- No monthly fees
- No hidden fees

**International Cards:**
- Additional 1% for cards issued outside US
- Currency conversion fees apply

**Subscriptions:**
- Same as one-time (2.9% + $0.30 per charge)
- Charged on each renewal

---

### Payment Limits

**Account Limits:**
- No monthly cap (process unlimited)
- Individual transaction max: $999,999.99

**Withdrawal Limits:**
- Depends on account history
- Starts around $2,000/day
- Increases over time with volume

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed

**Common Causes:**
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect card details
- Card declined by bank
- Address verification failed

**Customer Actions:**
- Try different card
- Contact their bank
- Use different payment method
- Check billing address matches card

---

### Stripe Connection Error

**Cause:** OAuth connection expired, account issue

**Fix:**
1. Settings > **Integrations** > **Stripe**
2. Click **Reconnect**
3. Re-authorize
4. Test with small payment

---

### Payout Delayed

**Common Causes:**
- Bank verification pending
- Unusual activity (fraud prevention)
- Holiday/weekend (no bank processing)

**Fix:**
- Wait 1-2 business days
- Check Stripe Dashboard for issues
- Contact Stripe support if >3 days

## Best Practices

### Clear Pricing

**Do:**
- Show total price upfront
- Explain what's included
- List recurring vs one-time clearly
- Show money-back guarantee

**Don't:**
- Hide fees until checkout
- Use confusing payment plan language
- Surprise with automatic renewals

---

### Optimize Checkout

**Tips:**
- Single-page checkout (less friction)
- Mobile-optimized (50% of payments)
- Guest checkout (no account required)
- Multiple payment methods (cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Trust badges (security, SSL)

**Conversion Rate Goal:** >60% (checkout initiated → completed)
