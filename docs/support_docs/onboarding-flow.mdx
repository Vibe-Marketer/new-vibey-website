# Getting Started with Vibey

Welcome to VibeOS! Here's what happens when you first sign up and how to get the most from your trial.

## Your Onboarding Journey

### Step 1: Vibey Awakens

When you first arrive, you'll meet Vibey—our AI marketing copilot.

**The Awakening Sequence:**
1. **The Spark** - Vibey appears as a small glowing sphere
2. **The Recognition** - Vibey realizes you're here
3. **The Awakening** - Energy builds as Vibey powers up
4. **The Burst** - Vibey's full power activates
5. **The Hello** - Vibey reveals a friendly smile
6. **The Greeting** - "VIBEY" appears, ready to help

**What to Do:**
- Click **TURN ON** to wake Vibey
- Watch the 13-second animation
- Get ready to start building

---

### Step 2: Tell Us Your Name

Vibey wants to know who you are.

**What We Ask:**
- First name
- Last name (optional)
- How you heard about us (optional)

**Why We Ask:**
- Personalize your experience
- Address you by name in emails
- Improve our service

---

### Step 3: Choose Your Trial

VibeOS offers two trial options. Pick the one that fits your needs.

### Free Trial (7 Days)

**Perfect for:**
- Testing VibeOS risk-free
- Exploring features quickly
- No credit card required

**What You Get:**
- **Full PRO access** for 7 days
- **50,000 AI words** during trial
- **10 published funnels**
- All Pro features unlocked
- Campaigns, sequences, integrations
- Custom domains (up to 5)
- Priority support

**After 7 Days:**
- Automatically downgrades to Free plan
- Keeps 3 published funnels (10 total drafts)
- AI words drop to 10,000/month
- All data retained
- Upgrade to Pro anytime

**Best For:**
- Quick evaluation
- No commitment
- Just exploring

### Paid Trial (30 Days)

**Perfect for:**
- Building and launching
- Extended evaluation time
- Serious about growth

**What You Get:**
- **Full PRO access** for 30 days
- **50,000 AI words** during trial
- **10 published funnels**
- All Pro features unlocked
- Campaigns, sequences, integrations
- Custom domains (up to 5)
- Priority support

**Cost:**
- **$1 for 30 days**
- Credit card required
- Auto-converts to $147/month after 30 days
- Cancel anytime to avoid charge

**After 30 Days:**
- Automatically converts to Pro at $147/month
- Full PRO access continues
- Cancel before 30 days to avoid charge
- If canceled, downgrades to Free plan

**Best For:**
- Building real campaigns
- Need more time
- Planning to go Pro

### Step 7: Create Your First Offer

Time to build! Vibey guides you through creating your first offer.

**What You'll Do:**
1. **Name your offer** - What are you selling?
2. **Describe it** - Tell Vibey about your product/service
3. **Add details** - Target audience, pricing, outcomes
4. **Generate** - AI creates complete offer document

**What You Get:**
- Power offer statement
- Buyer persona
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Competitive edge analysis
- Ready to use in funnels

**Time:** 10-15 minutes

## Trial FAQs

**Do I need a credit card for the free trial?**
No. The 7-day free trial requires no payment info.

**What happens after my trial ends?**
Free trial → Downgrades to Free plan automatically
Paid trial → Converts to $147/month Pro plan (cancel to avoid)

**Can I cancel during my trial?**
Yes. Cancel anytime. No penalties.

**Will I lose my data if I downgrade?**
No. All data stays. Only limits change (3 published funnels, 10K AI words).

**Can I upgrade from Free trial to Paid trial?**
Yes. Upgrade anytime during your 7-day free trial to get 30 days for $1.

**What's the special offer after onboarding?**
Limited-time offers may appear to extend or upgrade your trial. These are optional.

**Can I skip onboarding?**
No. Onboarding ensures your account is set up correctly and you understand trial terms.
