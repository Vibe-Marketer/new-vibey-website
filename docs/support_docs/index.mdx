## How It Works

1. **Create Your Offer** - Answer 6 questions. AI scrapes your URLs, analyzes your market, builds your offer document.
2. **Generate Brand Guidelines** - AI creates your brand identity. Colors, fonts, voice—all consistent.
3. **Build Your Funnel** - Pick a template. AI writes your copy. Customize what you need. Publish.
4. **Connect Your Domain** - Add your domain. Follow DNS instructions. Go live.

That's it.
